List of Cognitive Science Journals

Primary research articles and review aricles about cognition and intelligence are published in many journals. Here is a short list of journals where you might find review articles and empirical articles. Please note that this list is not exhaustive but simply a representative sample of journals we might encounter in class and you might consider looking through for your final projects.
